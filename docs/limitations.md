# Limitations

- NDVI/EVI are vegetation-condition proxies, not direct crop yield.
- Nightlights and electricity access are proxies for capacity, not complete infrastructure measures.
- Boundary changes must be harmonized before making time-series claims.
- Controlled-access survey data such as LSMS/UNPS or DHS should not be bundled in the repository.
- The action categories support planning review; they do not replace local expertise or formal policy authority.
