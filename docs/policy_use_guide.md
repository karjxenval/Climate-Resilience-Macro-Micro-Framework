# Policy use guide

The workflow produces four planning categories:

- **Act now**: high priority, lower uncertainty. Use for immediate planning review.
- **Measure first**: high priority, higher uncertainty. Use for targeted verification before major investment.
- **Monitor**: medium priority or unstable ranking. Use for short-cycle tracking.
- **Lower priority**: lower current evidence of combined climate-capacity risk.

The categories are not automatic funding decisions. They are a structured evidence register for review meetings.
